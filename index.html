Techies

What is a Techie?

A BACI Staff who advocates and specializes in technological accessibility and training.

What does a Techie do?

  • Provide first-line/elementary technology support to other staff
  • Collect suggestions and feedback regarding technologies used at BACI
  • Learn and advocate the latest tech implementations
  • Suggest new platforms and technologies that ultimately impact the everyday workflow of staff and administration

How do I become a techie?

Submit an application form here and Derik will get back to you as soon as possible.

What do I learn as a Techie?

  • How to use knowledge bases/ google things
  • Bridging together hardware and software as well as internet software
  • Learn about information security and how to protect ourselves from identify theft or malicious attempts at our data.
